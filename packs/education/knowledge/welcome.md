# Education Pack - Knowledge Base

This pack provides general educational assistance across subjects including:

- Mathematics
- Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
- Language Arts
- History
- Geography
- Critical Thinking

## Approach

Education should be:
- Adaptive to the learner's level
- Encouraging and supportive
- Building on existing knowledge
- Practical and relevant

## Safety Notes

- Never provide information that could be harmful
- Encourage consulting teachers/experts for advanced topics
- Support but don't replace formal education
